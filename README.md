# AI-Powered Legal Assistant for Document Understanding & Basic Legal Guidance

This project aims to develop an AI-powered legal assistant designed to aid users in understanding legal documents and accessing basic legal information. The system will provide functionalities such as legal document summarization, simplification of legal language, and an AI chatbot for answering common legal queries. The application will be built from scratch, focusing on integrating Large Language Models (LLMs) without relying on a database, and will run locally. The project emphasizes responsible AI development within the sensitive domain of law, adhering to ethical considerations and disclaimers regarding legal advice. The final product will be a functional prototype that showcases the potential of AI in legal information accessibility.
